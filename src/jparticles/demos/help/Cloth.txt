<h2> Description</h2>

This object is created by adding zero length springs along the white links
and nonzero length springs along their diagonals
(which are not visible in the simulation). The whole structure is under the influence of gravity.

<h2>Controls</h2>

<b><i>Particles</b></i>: The yellow particles are free to move. The red ones are fixed.
To fix-release a particle, press the right mouse button while the mouse pointer is over the particle.
To move a particle, drag the mouse while holding left mouse button."

<p><b><i>Space Bar</i></b>: Toggle Stop/Start the animation.</p>
