<h2>Description</h2>

This applet shows a snake. i.e a chain of particles with a stretch and bend potentials.

<h2>Controls </h2>

<b><i>Viscosity</i></b>: Set the value for the medium viscosity.
Don't forget to press the return key to activate the new value.


<p><b><i>Time-step</i></b> : Set the value for the time step.
 Don't forget to press the return key to activate the new value.</p>

<p><b><i>Particles</i></b> : The yellow particles are free to move. The red ones are fixed.
To fix-release a particle, press the right mouse button while the mouse pointer is over the
particle To move a particle drag the mouse while holding its left button.</p>

<p><b><i>Left slider</i></b>: Controls the stiffness of the stretch potential.</p>

<p><b><i>Right slider</i></b>: Controls the stiffness of the bending potential.</p>

<p><b><i>Space Bar</i></b>: Toggle Stop/Start the animation.</p>
